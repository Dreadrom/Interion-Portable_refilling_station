Delete Your BlueDiesel App Account

If you'd like to delete your BlueDiesel portable refill app account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data including transaction history, wallet balance, and profile information will be permanently deleted.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Transaction history and receipts
  • Wallet balance and top-up records
  • All associated account data

What We Keep (Legal Requirements)

For legal and regulatory compliance, we may retain:

  • Transaction records required for tax purposes (retained for 7 years as per Malaysian law)
  • Financial audit trails for payment reconciliation

These records are anonymized and cannot be linked back to your personal identity.

How to Request Account Deletion

Follow these steps:

  1. Email us from the email address registered to your app account
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include: Your full name and email address
  4. Send to: info@bluediesel.com.my

Processing Time

Your account deletion request will be processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been permanently deleted.
