### **Full Description** (4000 characters max)

```
BlueDiesel Refill Kiosk transforms AdBlue refueling into a seamless, contactless experience. Scan, dispense, and go — all from your smartphone.

🚛 FOR COMMERCIAL VEHICLE OPERATORS

BlueDiesel is designed for fleet managers, truck operators, and commercial vehicle owners who need fast, reliable AdBlue refueling without waiting for attendants or dealing with manual paperwork.

⚡ KEY FEATURES

• QR Code Scanning: Scan the station QR code to unlock the pump instantly
• Live Dispensing Monitor: Watch real-time volume and cost as you refuel
• Digital Wallet: Pre-load funds for faster checkout and better budget control
• Transaction History: Access all receipts and refueling records in one place
• Station Finder: Locate nearby BlueDiesel stations with live availability
• Secure Authentication: Your account and wallet are protected with industry-standard encryption
• Pre-Authorization: Set spending limits for controlled refueling sessions

🔐 SAFE & SECURE

Your payments are processed through secure gateways with full encryption. We never store your payment card details. All transactions are logged with timestamps and station references for complete transparency.

💳 PAYMENT OPTIONS

• Digital wallet top-ups via credit/debit card
• Pre-authorized dispensing with automatic deductions
• Instant transaction confirmations
• Download receipts directly to your device

📍 HOW IT WORKS

1. Register your account with email and password
2. Find a nearby BlueDiesel refill station
3. Top up your digital wallet
4. Scan the station QR code with your camera
5. Dispense AdBlue and watch the live meter
6. Transaction completes automatically
7. Download your receipt instantly

🏭 FOR FLEET MANAGERS

Perfect for managing multiple vehicles:
• Track all refueling transactions by vehicle
• Monitor AdBlue usage patterns
• Export transaction history for expense reports
• Set spending controls per user

🌍 EXPANDING NETWORK

We're launching in Malaysia with plans to expand across Southeast Asia. Check the app for the latest station locations near you.

📞 CUSTOMER SUPPORT

Need help? Contact our support team:
• Email: info@bluediesel.com.my

⚠️ ALPHA VERSION

This is an early access release. Some features like live station data require hardware deployment, which is currently in progress. Your feedback helps us improve!

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
```
