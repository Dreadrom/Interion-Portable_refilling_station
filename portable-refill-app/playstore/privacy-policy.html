Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

BlueDiesel Malaysia ("we", "our", "us") operates the portable refill app and kiosk services, including the BlueDiesel refill experience (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we disclose it, and your rights under applicable law.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, mobile number, email address, company details, vehicle details, and other information you provide when you register or contact us.
  • Location data: Approximate or precise location if you enable it, used to show nearby refilling stations and support transaction safety checks.
  • Transaction data: Dispensing records including station, nozzle, product, volume, cost, timestamps, payment references, wallet activity, stop reasons, receipts, and support or dispute records.
  • Device and technical data: Device identifiers, IP address, app logs, session metadata, and fraud-prevention signals used to secure the service.
  • Website usage data: Cookies and similar technologies where used on our websites or hosted policy pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your access.
  • To authorize payments, secure your wallet, process dispensing transactions, and generate receipts.
  • To show nearby stations and support station operations.
  • To investigate fraud, misuse, disputes, chargebacks, safety incidents, and unauthorized activity.
  • To provide customer support and respond to requests or complaints.
  • To comply with legal, tax, accounting, audit, safety, and regulatory obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may disclose relevant information to:

  • Payment processors, e-wallet providers, and banks to authorize, settle, reverse, or investigate payments.
  • Station operators and service providers where needed to operate dispensing services or investigate incidents.
  • Cloud, hosting, analytics, compliance, insurance, and professional service providers acting on our instructions or for lawful business purposes.
  • Regulators, law enforcement, auditors, and courts when required by law or reasonably necessary to protect our rights or users.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes collected or as required by law. Transaction, accounting, tax, and audit records may be retained for at least 7 years. Some information may also be retained where needed to resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent fraud, or comply with legal obligations.

5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Request withdrawal of consent for certain optional processing activities.
  • Request account deletion, subject to retention obligations and lawful business needs.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. Withdrawal of consent may affect our ability to continue providing some services where the processing is necessary to run your account, wallet, or dispensing transactions.

6. Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, disclosure, or loss. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

The App is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Cross-Border Processing

Where our service providers process personal data outside Malaysia, we will take reasonable steps to ensure the data is protected in a manner comparable to applicable legal requirements.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our websites and hosted pages may use cookies and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, improve usability, and generate aggregate analytics. You can manage cookies through your browser settings or available consent tools.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted in the App or on our website, together with the updated effective date.
